What is DOLE SOFT SERVE® Mix? It’s a dry soft serve mix that makes it fast and easy for you to whip up better-for-you treats with a variety of dairy-free true-to-fruit fruit flavors, including Pineapple, Mango, Lemon, Orange, Raspberry, Strawberry, Lime, Cherry, and Watermelon. DOLE SOFT SERVE® Mix is a great way to offer patrons a lighter, better-for-you frozen treat. And because it’s made with no artificial colors and is bursting with the sweet fruit flavors customers crave, your sales will take a cool turn upward.
