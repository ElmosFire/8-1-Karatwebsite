Benefits of working at Karat® by Lollicup™

Karat® is a premium manufacturing company of foodservice disposables and a complete solution for all specialty beverage businesses. We produce paper and plastic cups, dessert cups and food containers, straws, foodservice gloves and more. We offer a fast paced environment and believe in the success of our employees by supporting them and growing their skill set.

Corporate Internships

Just getting started? Our interns experience life at Karat® in a hands-on environment like no other. Whether you’re an undergrad student or freshly out of college, we’re interested in what you have to offer. Our paid internship opportunities will not only give you a window into working at Karat®, but enable you to use what you’ve learned, expand your knowledge, and benefit from invaluable on-the-job experience. Interns work as an integral part of project teams and develop initiatives alongside staff as well as opportunities to interact with Karat executive leadership. Explore our corporate internships and discover how your skills and passions align with ours at Karat®.
